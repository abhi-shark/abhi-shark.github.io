Senior Design Project: Injection Molding Energy Reclamation

Designed and manufactured a prototype waste heat reclamation system capturing thermal energy from the cooling stage of injection Molding to be reused in the heating stage, reducing process energy consumption.

Lubrication Simulation Project

ICE Engine Simulation using GT-POWER

Simulated naturally aspirated, turbocharged and supercharged ICE engines (gasoline and diesel), along with BEVs, PHEVs and FCEVs using GT-POWER, and compared the results to laboratory runs using a 3.3L Duratec V6 gasoline and a 3.0L Powerstroke V6 diesel engine.
